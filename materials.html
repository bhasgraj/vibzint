Our Premium Materials & Varieties

We use only high-end, termite-proof, and moisture-resistant building materials tested for long-lasting performance in Tamil Nadu’s tropical climate.

1. Plywoods & Base Varieties

The foundation of all wooden work. We do not use cheap materials that warp under humidity.

  • BWR Plywood (Boiling Water Resistant): Ideal for living room cabinets and structural wardrobes.
  • BWP Plywood (Boiling Water Proof / Marine Grade): 100% mandatory for our kitchen base units and bathroom wet areas. Termite and borer protection guaranteed.

2. Modern Finishes & Surfaces

  • Sun mica / Premium Laminates: Available in high-gloss, matte, and textured wood finishes. Highly scratch-proof.
  • Acrylic Finishes: Provides a mirror-like, ultra-modern luxury sheen perfect for premium kitchen shutters.
  • WPC & UPVC: Wood Polymer Composites. 100% waterproof alternatives used for structural frames and damp walls.

3. Countertops, Marbles & Tiles

  • Quartz & Granite: High-impact surfaces designed to handle stains, heavy vessels, and intense Indian cooking.
  • Premium Marble & Vitrified Tiles: Luxury flooring textures and gorgeous seamless patterns.

4. Intelligent Kitchen Storage Solutions

Maximized layouts so you don't have to bend down or struggle for corner spaces.

  • Kitchen Base Units: Heavy-weight load capacity drawers with smooth soft-close channels.
  • Corner Solutions: Magic corners and Lemans trays to transform hidden corners into useful storage.
  • Tall Units & Pantries: Bulk grocery towers with elegant pull-out baskets.

5. Ceilings, Doors & Intelligent Illumination

  • False Ceiling Materials: Designer Gypsum and grid options integrated with ambient LED spot-lighting.
  • Modern Lightings: Warm profile strip lights, chandeliers, and motion-sensor wardrobe lighting profiles.
  • Windows & Doors: Heavy-duty, sound-insulating UPVC window frameworks and solid-core designer main doors.
